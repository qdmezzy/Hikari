# Founding 25 invitation copy

Use a unique invitation link for each person. Send it privately and replace
`[name]` with something personal so the note feels like an invitation, not a
campaign.

Hey [name] — I’m inviting a very small group of people who genuinely care
about anime and the kind of community Hikari could become.

I’d love you to be one of Hikari’s **Founding 25**: the first 25 approved
community members, each with a permanent numbered badge, a private Discord
space, and a vote on selected features. It is not first-come-by-signup; I’m
sharing these invitations personally with people whose taste and perspective
I value.

If that sounds like something you would enjoy helping shape, your private,
single-use invitation is below. No pressure if the timing is not right.
